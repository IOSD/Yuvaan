WORD WAR

The Conventional Debate Competition

Situation to solution has a path full of heat, Come join, boil, heat up!We bring the old debating syndrome with new topics infested.Get Ready.Falling on the lines of a conventional debate, this event will aim at a healthy exchange of ideas between participants in favor of and participants against the motion. The motion will revolve around a socially relevant, thought-provoking yet interesting issue. Judgment will be made according to the content, fluency, speaking skills, and ability to connect with the audience.

QUIZOTICA

A test of General Knowledge

Do you read the newspaper everyday? Are you The next DerekO'Brien?
The quiz competition will function according to the elementary rules of quizzing competitions. The rules of procedure in detail will be up to to the discretion of the Quiz-Master. Teams of two will be allowed to participate.

ADD MAD

An Advertisement Enactment Competition

Chandavati Churan or Maybe a chocolate having chilli flavor!What's your take? This competition aims tickle the creative and spontaneity skills of the participants. Participation will be in teams of 3-5. Product to be advertised will be given 24 hours prior to the event. Teams with the best mix of creativity, humor, effective presentation and enactment, with the most convincing advertisement will be awarded.

KAVI SAMMELAN

Performance by Professionals

From Drowning daughter of Ullin's to the Jhansi ki RaniWhat is a literary fest without a poetic performance.Professional poets will be invited to perform for the audience.

RHYME TYM

A Theatrical Poetry Extravaganza

Ruko mat,jaao or Ruko, mat jaao.... How different aren't they?
The competition is intended to bring out the best in peopleâ€™s poetry skills. The content as well as the manner of speaking will play a vital role in deciding the winner. Connection with the audience will be tested as well. The event will be divided into two rounds. Prelims will consist of the interested candidates mailing a 45-60 second video clip based on which short-listing will be done. The shortlisted candidates will be required to come prepared with their poetry for performing. Candidates guilty of plagiarism will be disqualified. Decision of the Judge will be final and binding.

FOODIE-LICIOUS

A food critiquing event

We are the foodies! Can you also tell the breed of pepper on tasting? Here we are! An event with a unique and exciting idea of making participants taste scrumptious food, based on which they will be required to write a 500-700 word long blog entry talking about its taste, appearance, nutritional value etc. Judgment will be based on the style and content of writing.

PANORAMA

An Interpretation Competition for the Creative Mind

The artist will be required to draw, paint, and sketch the given theme on a chart paper. The artwork will then be shown to the speaker who will have to interpret its underlying idea after looking at it for 1 minute. Judgment will be based on quality of artwork and proximity of theme interpreted to that of the given.
